Personal Information

Amelie Lens (born 31 May 1990) is a Belgian electronic music DJ, record producer, former model and co-owner of the Lenske record label. She is signed to the Pan-Pot imprint Second State records, with whom she has relaesed several singles and EPs, the most successful to date being 2017's Contradiction EP. She has also recorded for the Drumcode and Elevate labels. Her live sets tend towards bass-heavy minimal techno.
